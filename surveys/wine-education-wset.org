* Scope and Method

This survey provides comprehensive reference material for wine education,
structured around the Wine & Spirit Education Trust (WSET) curriculum
from Level 1 through Level 4 (Diploma). It covers grape varieties and
vine biology, winemaking processes, world wine regions, tasting
methodology and food pairing, and the business, classification, and
history of wine.

Method: web-accessible sources --- WSET official materials, wine education
platforms, trade publications, academic references, and industry databases.
Sources cited with URLs throughout. Each section is tagged with the WSET
level at which the material becomes examinable.
